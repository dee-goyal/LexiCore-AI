# Knowledge Base

This folder contains legal documents, constitutional texts, and other reference materials for the Legal AI Agent.

## Structure

Organize your legal documents in the following way:

```
knowledge/
├── constitution/
│   ├── constitution_full_text.txt
│   ├── amendments.txt
│   └── fundamental_rights.txt
├── laws/
│   ├── criminal_law/
│   ├── civil_law/
│   ├── corporate_law/
│   └── other/
├── case_law/
│   ├── supreme_court/
│   └── high_courts/
└── reference/
    ├── legal_terminology.txt
    └── legal_procedures.txt
```

## How to Add Documents

1. **Constitution Documents**
   - Add full constitution text
   - Include amendments
   - Add explanations of fundamental rights

2. **Laws and Statutes**
   - Organize by category (criminal, civil, corporate, etc.)
   - Include act text and amendments
   - Add relevant rules and regulations

3. **Case Law**
   - Add landmark judgments
   - Include case summaries
   - Organize by court level

4. **Reference Materials**
   - Legal terminology
   - Procedural guides
   - Legal forms and templates

## Supported Formats

- `.txt` - Plain text files
- `.pdf` - PDF documents
- `.md` - Markdown files
- `.json` - Structured legal data

## Tips

- Use clear, descriptive filenames
- Include metadata (date, source, citation)
- Keep files organized by topic
- Update regularly with new laws and judgments

## Integration

The AI agents use these documents to:
- Answer legal queries accurately
- Provide citations and references
- Explain laws in context
- Analyze constitutional provisions

## Resources

To populate this knowledge base, consider:
- Government law websites
- Official gazette publications
- Supreme Court website
- Legal databases (Indian Kanoon, etc.)
- Law commission reports

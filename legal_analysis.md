# Legal Analysis Results

This file will store the results of legal analyses performed by the AI agents.

## How to Use

Results from each analysis will be automatically appended here with:
- Timestamp
- Query
- Agent findings
- Final advisory

## Example Format

```markdown
---
Date: 2025-11-27
Query: What does Article 21 of the Constitution mean?
---

### Research Findings
[Legal research results...]

### Constitutional Analysis
[Constitutional analysis...]

### Legal Analysis
[Detailed legal analysis...]

### Final Advisory
[Comprehensive advisory...]

---
```
